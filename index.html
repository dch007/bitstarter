Movie Pointer is a web app that answers one simple question for you:

What is the best opportunity to watch a great movie right now?

A question that is not easily answered, because is has three parts:

  • ...which movie theater is nearby...
  • ...that shows a movie right in time...
  • ...that you will you probably like and enjoy?!
Movie Pointer knows the answer - so you can go to the movies offhanded without having to do research first - the app movie goers have been waiting for!

Movie Pointer needs you: This mobile-friendly web app brings geolocation, movie programs and movie critic's recommendations together - if you can't wait to use it and leave the movie-research-hassle behind, please support this project. Thank you very much. You will love the result.
